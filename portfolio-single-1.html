The Society Customer Connect App is a custom-built solution tailored exclusively for Riverview City Construction LTD, a distinguished leader in the real estate sector. This app is meticulously crafted utilizing cutting-edge technologies such as Angular 12, Ionic 5, and customized SCSS for design aesthetics. It serves as a dedicated platform for residents who have invested in properties within the Riverview City Township in Pune.

Built on Angular 12 and TypeScript 4.2, the application ensures optimal performance and maintainability. The use of Ionic 5 facilitates cross-platform compatibility, making the app accessible to a diverse audience on both iOS and Android devices. The unique SCSS design adds a personalized touch, reflecting the distinctive aesthetics of the Riverview City development.

Key Features:

1. Document Notification:

Timely notifications for all generated documents, from demand letters to receipts, ensuring transparency and customer awareness. Comprehensive view of payment details and schedules for convenient access to financial information.

2. Sales Team Enhancement:

Elevates the performance of the sales team through the introduction of a referral lead module, fostering customer engagement and loyalty.

3. Offer Section:

Enhances the customer experience with a dedicated offer section, providing exclusive deals and promotions directly through the app.
