Housing Society Customer Connect App

UI Redesign

© Reserved to Riverview City Construction LTD

Scroll
Elevating User Experience through Gamification and Enhanced Functionality
Project Date Jan 15th, 2024
Role UX Research, UI Design, Development
Company Name Tribe Appsoft LLP
Client Riverview City Construction LTD

As a UI/UX designer Using Adobe XD, I have had the privilege of spearheading the redesign of our Society Customer Connect app, aimed at enhancing user engagement and satisfaction. Through meticulous UX research and iterative design processes, we have reimagined the app's interface and functionality to create a seamless and enjoyable user experience.

Key Design Features:

1. Gamification with Points System:

Have introduced a gamification element to the app by implementing a points system. Users can now earn in-app points by engaging with various features and completing specific tasks, fostering a sense of achievement and incentivizing continued app usage.

2. Z-Pattern Layout:

The app's design follows a Z-pattern layout, guiding users' attention along a natural viewing path from the top-left corner to the bottom-right corner. This layout enhances usability and ensures that important content and functionalities are easily accessible and prioritized.

3. Simplified Social Features:

Simplified the social features within the app to focus primarily on liking posts. Users can now like posts shared within the community to earn points and show appreciation for shared content. This streamlined approach encourages user engagement while maintaining simplicity and ease of use.

4. Optimized Element Placement:

Each element in the app's UI has been strategically placed to optimize user flow and navigation. By adhering to principles of visual hierarchy and consistency, we've ensured that users can intuitively navigate through the app and access desired functionalities without friction.
